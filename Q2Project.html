LANDMARKS IN THE PHILIPPINES

The Philippines is filled with numerous beautiful sights and places to see. There are lots to see and talk about out there, but for now, here are 3 well-known destinations and places in the Philippines to see:

Banaue Rice Terraces

Banaue Rice Terraces is mainly famous for it's breathtaking view. It is located on Ifugao's mountains and is claimed by locals to be built by hand to have a place to plant rice on thousands of years ago.

Chocolate Hills

Chocolate Hills is located at Bohol, from the name itself, this famous destination is famous for it's hills turning brown during the dry season, making it look like chocolate truffles (Or chocolate pudding in others' perspective.)

Calle Crisologo

This is a sacred historical place, known for it's beautiful history, carriage rides, and its preserved Spanish architecture. Calle Crisologo is located in Vigan, this place used to be known as a trading route and the home of many historical events. Until now, locals try to preserve it's architecture, for others to see
