NOLI ME TANGERE LIST OF CHARACTERS
Kapitan Tiago
  • The gobernadorcillo of San Diego who claims to be a Spaniard/Kastila.
  • He is the husband of Pia Alba and the one who raised Maria Clara alongside his cousin tiya Isabel.
  • Designed his house with religious items and portraits to make him seem religious, but instead of worshiping those, he worships the dozens of saint figures he has in one of the house's rooms.
Padre Damaso
  • The ill-mannered, ruthless, short-tempered, conceited priest of the novel and the curate/pastor of San Diego.
  • He the main antagonist and also the biological father of Maria Clara, disguising himself as the acting godfather.
  • After Don Rafael's burial, a grave digger was instructed to move the body to another cemetary by Padre Damaso, but was instead chucked into a river bank.
Pilosopo Tasio
  • The old man who was considered as a "lunatic" in the novel because of his peculiar and "extreme" ideas.
  • After the death of his wife and mother, he immersed himself with his studies, buying books and ignoring his estate and job until he was bankrupt, leading people to the thought that he was a crazy lunatic.
  • He later on became acquainted with his neighbor Sisa and her two sons Basilio and Crispin.
